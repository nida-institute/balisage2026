# The Kairos Project and Scripture Pipelines

*A Nida Institute initiative for global biblical scholarship*

---

The Kairos Project exists to raise up scholars worldwide, working together to produce high-quality, freely licensed biblical resources for Bible translation teams, study communities, and readers in every culture.

Scripture Pipelines is the infrastructure that makes this possible at scale.

They aren't separate things. Scripture Pipelines is built to serve The Kairos Project. Every pipeline, every dataset, every freely licensed output exists to serve the mission: fully equipping the global church by producing resources together with communities worldwide — resources they can adapt to their own needs.
